Who We Are

SLS Microfinance Bank is founded with focus on the financially unserved and underserved. We are people oriented, with the aim of providing all with basic financial services in an innovative and easily accessible manner.

We are people oriented, with the aim of providing all with basic financial services in an innovative and easily accessible manner. We strive to be the leading and most innovative financial services provider to all, at world class standard.

Need a loan? Have no worries. At SLS microfinance Bank we provide instant loan services. Our loans are available for your personal, business use. Are you a public servant our SLS Stress free loan and SLS Salary Advance loan will meet your needs and obligations. Likewise for those working in private establishments our SLS Salary Advance loan will also meet your needs. Are you a business owner our SLS SME loan products will help you finance your business.

Our accounts are tailored to cover a wide range of customer’s financial needs from savings to current, corporate and hybrid accounts. This helps us to ensure no one is left out in financial inclusion.

Omololu Akinjide Adewalure

Chief Operations Officer

Omololu Akinjide Adewalure is the Chief operations officer. He has had 9 years working experience in banking operations. He started his banking experience with Skye bank as a customer experience management personnel and then moved to Guaranty Trust Bank in 2010. He has functioned at various times in different capacities in banking operations and back up Head of operations GTBank until he joined Accelerex Microfinance Bank. He is a member Nigerian Institute of management and Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria.

Anara Chinyere Grace

Head, Operations

Grace Anara is the head Banking Operations. She is a customer service expert, with 10 years banking operations experience. She has functioned in various capacities in Fortis microfinance Bank. She left Fortis Microfinance as Head Banking operations with 16 branches under her supervision to join the Accelerex Microfinance bank Team.

TEMITOPE AKINYEMI

Chairman of the Board

Temitope AKINYEMI is an accomplished Financial and Investment Adviser, Transaction Adviser and Public Private Partnership (PPP) advisor. He has over 19 years’ experience in finance. He had worked for the Power Team at the Bureau of Public Enterprise (BPE) under the Presidency in Nigeria. He also was one of three Portfolio Managers at Nigeria Sovereign Investment Authority (NSIA). While there, had direct responsibility for the Authority’s largest portfolio of PPP toll road projects including Second Niger Bridge and Lagos Ibadan Express road. He is a chartered financial analyst also an MBA Dual Concentration in Finance and Strategy, Yale School of Management, USA
